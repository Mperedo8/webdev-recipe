Pecan Pie Recipe

Ingredients

  • Pecans
  • Crust
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp of corn syrup

Methods

  1. Roll out the dough and form the crust to the pan
  2. Mix the butter and the sugar
  3. add the 2 eggs and whisk
  4. Put the mixture in the pan on the crust
  5. Place in fridge
  6. After cooled, put in oven at 350 for 45 min
  7. Take out and enjoy
